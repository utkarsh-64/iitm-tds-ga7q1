Financial Formulas: Profitability

Our Net Profit Margin is a key indicator of our efficiency.

The formula we use is:

$$ \text{Net Profit Margin} = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Revenue}} \times 100\% $$

For Q3 2025: $ \frac{\$33M}{\$150M} \times 100\% = 22\% $

To be precise, our net profit margin is calculated by dividing net income by revenue. This quarter, with \$33 million in net income against \$150 million in revenue, we achieved a healthy margin of 22%.
